Dark Souls Warrior Strength Build

The warrior strength build is a classic choice for those who want to play as a heavily armored tank in Dark Souls. This build focuses on increasing strength, endurance, and armor to create a character that can take a lot of damage while dealing out massive damage of their own.

Attributes:

For a strength build, you want to focus on increasing the strength and endurance attributes. A good rule of thumb is to have a ratio of 40 strength to 40 endurance, with the rest of your levels going into vitality.

Armor:

Wearing heavy armor is key for a strength build. The havel's set, Smough's set, and the giants set are all good choices for increasing defense and resistances. Make sure to upgrade your armor as you progress through the game to stay ahead of the curve.

Weapons:

For a strength build, you want to focus on using weapons that scale with strength. Greatswords, ultra greatswords, and axes are all good choices. Make sure to upgrade your weapons as you progress through the game to increase damage output.

Rings:

Wearing rings that increase strength and endurance is important for a strength build. The Ring of Steel Protection and the Knight's Ring are both good choices for increasing strength and endurance.

Gameplay Tips:

When playing as a strength build, make sure to take your time and plan your attacks. Don't be afraid to block and dodge to conserve stamina, and make sure to use your heavy attacks to break through enemy defenses.

Conclusion:

The warrior strength build is a classic choice for those who want to play as a heavily armored tank in Dark Souls. With a focus on strength, endurance, and armor, this build can withstand a lot of damage while dealing out massive damage of its own. Just remember to take your time, plan your attacks, and upgrade your equipment as you progress through the game.
